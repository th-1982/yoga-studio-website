Lucy I. -D

Vinyasa Yoga,Hatha Yoga and Yin Yoga

Lucy is the founder of our yoga studio. She was a gymnast and a dancer. She had health challenges which affected her mental state. As a result, she took a deeper look at her life and made a decision to prioritze her health for the better. she became a yoga instructor to inspire others who might be going through similar challenges like she was. She is experienced in Vinyase yoga, Hatha yoga and Yin yoga. She cannot wait to meet you and show you how to free your mind and body and immerse yourself in this beautiful yoga experience.
